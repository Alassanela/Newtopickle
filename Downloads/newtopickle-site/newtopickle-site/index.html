You can, but it is not safe. Running shoes are built for forward movement. Pickleball moves you side to side, and a tall running sole can roll your ankle when you cut sideways. Court or tennis shoes are the safe choice. Read why here.
The kitchen is the zone about 7 feet from the net on each side. You cannot hit the ball out of the air while standing in it. It is the rule beginners break most. Full explanation here.
You will feel comfortable in about a month of regular play, maybe faster. Pickleball improves quickly at the start because the court is small and the movements repeat. The soft game near the net takes longer, but you can win points long before you master it.
